MEET OUR CEO


GERANTZ Nigeria Limited Engineers are carefully selected and well qualified with extensive combined total experience of 70 years in the oil and gas industry. The MD, Engr. Emmanuel Osisi established GERANTZ Nigeria Limited and serves as its CEO. Engr. Emmanuel Osisi has over 15 years' progressive experience in the oil and gas industry. Our engineers are fully conversant with both Nigerian and International Oil & Gas Companies (IOCs) standards and have successfully completed project for Halliburton, Vita Construction Engineering company, Orascom Construction Industries, Mikano International limited Etc.


COMPANY REGISTERED ACTIVITIES:


Oil Field Services, Pipeline Construction, Sand Blasting & Painting, Civil Work Construction, Project Management / Consultancy, (Bids) Supervision, Corrosion Control Services & Electrical


GERANTZ Nigeria Limited is a full supporting services company providing business in the oil and gas industry, power sector, marine sector as well as government sector.

GERANTZ NIGERIA LIMITED was duly incorporated in Nigeria in 1989 with RC:126748. The company is a private limited liability company currently exploring the vast business opportunities in the Nigeria Oil and Gas Sector. The Chairman of the company is Engineer Emmanuel Osisi. (A Trained Marine Engineer)

Onshore Pipeline / storage facility (Major Category - Upstream)
